Site Plan for WDD231 Project

Site Name

The name I have chosen for my site will be called "Bluey's Beauty." The business is inspired by my current line of work. The characters and information will be inspired by the show "Bluey." I wanted to combine my willingness to learn and involving my children for family time.

Site Purpose

The site will give information to perspective clients about services offered by this hair salon, a chance to book an appointment, and learn about who the stylists are.
